Overview

About Us

KAHANI BUFFET is a premier entertainment platform offering a unique glimpse into the lives and minds of renowned personalities from India and worldwide. Through our curated content, including stories, films, and documentaries, you'll gain insights into their thoughts, lifestyles, and opinions on their audience. Stay connected for an enriched experience tailored to your interests.

Our Journey

Our platform is designed to cater to a diverse audience globally, offering content that resonates with viewers and listeners from India and beyond. Since launching in 2025, we've been working diligently to fulfill our vision. Our upcoming schedule includes two podcasts with renowned Bollywood personalities this month, followed by a series of engaging content, including films, podcasts, documentaries, and interviews, all planned through March 2026. We're committed to this long-term journey and are excited to share our progress with our audience.

Our Vision

KAHANI BUFFET is an exciting new channel that's all about sparking creativity and entertainment. Our mission is to bring you closer to the world of Bollywood by sharing the personal stories of its stars. We'll feature films that are not just entertaining but also showcase artistic brilliance and creativity.

Our music section will be a treasure trove of soulful Hindi and English tracks, as well as folk music that connects you to your heritage. We'll also be exploring the world of politics, giving you a glimpse into the goals and motivations of our leaders. And, if you're interested in fitness, you'll love our athlete features, where they'll share their secrets to staying fit and their thoughts on societal wellness. Kahani Buffet is a startup that launched in 2025, and we're committed to making a mark in the world of entertainment.
